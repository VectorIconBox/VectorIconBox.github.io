************************Feature List******************
* Export high resolution png from vector types, in @1x,@2x,@3x, all in one time.
* Thousands of high quality free vector icons, all are searchable.
* Resize/ Color the icons you want.
* Export images of @1x.png, @2x.png, @3x.png in batch
* Export image in format of PNG/JPEG/jp2/TIFF
* Shortcut for Export(CMD+S), Copy(CMD+C)
* Copy icon to clipboard
